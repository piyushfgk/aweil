  • DHANUSH

    Dhanush is a state of the art gun system that has proved to be one of the best in class you can engage stationary and moving targets both day and night, based on a unified cycling system it can strike targets in the range of 38 kilometres & best of all 85% of business part it is a true make in India effort. With Dhanush, India has developed the capability manufacturing 155mm calibre Gun.

  • KAVACH

    KAVACH is a fast response anti-missile naval decoy system back to back self defence against radar guided enemy missiles using tactical chat it is available in two versions for long and short distance deployment and its wholly made in India.

  • ASSAULT RIFLE 7.62 X 51

    Assault rifle 7.62 mm X51. It is highly lethal weapon that can engage targets beyond 400 meters. Versatile by design, the weapon can be fitted with telescopic sight and reflex. Specifically developed for the Indian army and paramilitary forces, this product is 100% Indian in origin.

  • MULTI GRENADE LAUNCHER

    Multi Grenade Launcher popularly known as MGL 40 mm. This grenade launcher is perfect for counter insurgency and low intensity conflict deployable in built up and forest areas. Six shots can be fired in revolver operation 100% indigenous. MGL have been supplied to Indian armed forces and paramilitary forces.

  • MULTI SHELL LAUNCHER

    Multi Shell Launcher popularly known as MSL 38 mm. This is a force multiplier anti-riot shoulder fired weapon. MSL can fire six shots in rapid succession fires, tear smoke shells rubber bullets and batons. It is 100% indigenous MSL have been supplied to paramilitary forces and state police organisations.

  • UNDER BARREL GRENADE LAUNCHER

    Under Barrel Grenade Launcher popularly known as UBG 40 mm has a low weight low recoil long range and accurate. A soldier can fire up to 400 meters without changing the firing posture. It is ideal for use with rifles of 5.56 mm and AK 47 times 100% indigenous more than 21,000 UBG have been supplied to Indian armed forces and paramilitary forces.

CMD's Message

I extend a very warm Welcome to all the Officers, Staff and Workmen to the newly incorporated Advanced Weapons and Equipment India Limited (AWEIL), primarily engaged in Manufacture and Supply of Weapon Systems, Small Arms, Tank Gun Articles and Ammunition Components.

We are starting our journey with an Order Value of INR 5000 Cr approximately for Supplies to Armed Forces, Ministry of Home Affairs, Defence Public Sector Undertakings (DPSU) and Civil Trade. In addition, we are likely to receive an order for AK-203 Rifles which will be finalised soon. Further, we also expect more orders through few RFPs in which we have participated.

Today, as we are stepping into a new phase and beginning to function as a DPSU, I earnestly urge all the officers, staff and workmen to continue to work together with full dedication and commitment and quickly adapt ourselves to the new changing and challenging scenario.

Though, there may be many challenges which we may have to face, we have all the Strengths and Capabilities, Skills, Resources to meet all the Challenges, in the form of great experience, knowledge, Skilled, Experienced, Vibrant, Capable and committed workforce, state of the art Manufacturing facilities and Very Large Product Base.

I am confident that with the synergetic approach of each workmen, staff & officers and support of all Unions and Associations, we will definitely be able to overcome all the challenges in front of us and scale greater heights and will emerge as a Vibrant, Self-Sustained, Professionally managed, Dominant Weapon Producer and continue to work towards national goal of enhanced Self Reliance in Defence Production.

So, Let's take this transition positively and adapt ourselves quickly to new working systems and maintain High Morale, Team work, Harmonious Industrial Relations and unity among all levels.

Wishing all the workmen, staff and officers of AWElL and their families a very bright future.

Date: 01st Oct' 2021

Rajesh Choudhary

Chairman and Managing Director
